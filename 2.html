MARC VELAYO

WHAT SHOULD I CHOOSE?

Net Art, 2019. (Art 74)

Boba or Bubble tea is a popular tea-based drink. It originated from Taiwan and is being sold at a lot of countries.
Here in the Bay Area, a lot of Boba shops have opened. From Pekoe to Tastea, these shops have become a popular hangout
spot. My experiences with purchasing boba is heavily connected with a lot of memories I've made with my friends.
From taking the bus to a Boba shop with my high school friends to driving to the farthest Boba shop with my college peers,
It has definitely become a symbol of friendship and nostalgia.
The net art I created showcases various choices that will lead the viewer into choosing a Boba store in the end.
Each chosen store will feature a drink that will surely make the viewer crave for Boba.
