MARC VELAYO

I MISSED YOU

I missed you, Blender, 2019. 0:10sec (Art 103)

Have you experienced missing someone? Your emotions, especially
sadness hugging you as you go on your day? Have you ever felt a
deep connection with another person, but it doesn’t turn out the
way you wanted it to be? My piece-- I missed you-- is about every
person who comes home to their loved one, finding out that this
person has left. This person left either physically or mentally-- losing
the connection they once had.
I used 3D models of airplanes as my personal experiences of
leaving are deeply connected to the concept of planes. I am an
immigrant who left my home country in 2012. Riding in a plane
meant two things for me: I’m coming home or I’m saying my goodbyes.
This piece was created in Blender for my ART 103 class.
