HELLO, MY NAME IS ANXIETY

Hello, My Name is Anxiety, Digital Video, 2018. 3:07 mins (Art 75)

Mental health is one of the things that our society needs to prioritize. We encounter various
individuals everyday, and we can never tell what's going on with their lives.
Emotions are hard to express especially if one's vulnerability is on the line. Imagine walking down
the street, passing different people. Do you know if they are okay? If they are doing well?
One's physical appearance and reaction are not enough basis to create a conclusion that they are well.
This video will be tackling the stigma surrounding mental health, mainly anxiety. It features Mika Manalastas,
a friend who's dealing with the effects of anxiety. How does she cope with it? How does
she express it?
