MARC VELAYO

WITHOUT YOU

Without You, Digital Video, 2019. 4:33min (Art 105)

Losing someone is painful. Losing someone is difficult. Losing someone is one of the
hardest things that a person can deal with. It can leave a person in the void-- having no
energy or motivation to keep moving forward. But sometimes memories help. Memories are
reminders of happiness. Memories are reminders of love, care and warmth. Recollections of
the past can help someone to move forward and to love again.
This piece features a narrative between a couple who lost the connection they once had.
They spent their days together, but what happens if one of them falls apart?
