MARC VELAYO

LOOK UP

Look Up, Digital Video, 2018. 2:03min (Art 75)

Technology has impacted our lives in a lot of ways. From making the simplest tasks easier
to connecting every human being around the world. It plays an important role in our
society-- but it has its own positive and negative effects
that has heavily influenced our way living. It has brought a new way of communication, and the
most common way is through the use of our phones.
We all spend a lot of time on our phones, looking down and not realizing the amount of time
wasted on social media, games or other forms of distraction that our phones provide.
We forget to look up to the reality of life-- the things that are happening around us, the
things that was deemed important before. While taking actions on our phones-- swiping up, down,
left and right, do we still remember to take real action in our lives?
I asked SJSU students around campus to perform in the video. The camera I used was a Canon T6i.
