MARC VELAYO

I SAW YOUR PHOTO ONLINE

I Saw Your Photo Online, Digital Video, 2018. 0:10sec (Art 75)

Social media has impacted and influenced a lot of lives. It is used for communication and
expression, giving people the power to post their lives. People can share their photos,
videos and whatever medium they can use to convey their voice. But, with this powerful
tool comes a lot of negative effects especially when people use it to express hatred.
This piece shows the effects of how social media can ruin and destroy someone’s life. Mika
attends a party and gets intoxicated. The following day, her best friend finds out that
someone posted explicit photos of her online. What’s going to happen?
This piece features a side by side video with a conversation from a mobile application
called Messenger and clips from Mika’s day.
