HAPPY JOLLIBEE

Happy Jollibee, 3D, 2019. (Art 74)

Jollibee is a great example of how Filipino Culture is heavily integrated within
the United States. It is the most famous Filipino fast food chain, and has numerous
locations around the nation. It also represents how fast food places are a big part of the American culture.
For one of the 3D models I created for Art 74, I used the face of the Jollibee logo.
I initially created the model on Minecraft, and printed it using the 3D printer at school.
